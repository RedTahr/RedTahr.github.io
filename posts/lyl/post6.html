LVL Blog challenge Post 6

What difference do I want to make?

Help restore a sense of community that has been lost in western culture.

How? I'm not sure yet.

Western cultural traditions dictate lots of unhelpful things. Such as if you're pregnant, don't talk about it til after 12 weeks when the higher risk of misfortune has passed. This means that if you miscarry for example, then no one will know, so you're dealing with this intangible loss with at best your partner to console you. Even if the pregnancy goes fine you're still keeping a secret during one of the more commonly challenging (morning sickness anyone?) trimesters.
